Florence

At A Glance

Florence is a small city that packs a punch. At the heart of the city is the Duomo of Florence with most of the other tourist attractions relatively close by. With a lot to see in a relatively small area, you will probably run into most, if not all, the historic sites just walking around the downtown. Be sure to cross the Ponte Vecchio bridge to and sit at the Piazza le Michelangelo for a beautiful view of the city.
