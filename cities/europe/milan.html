Milan

At A Glance

Milan is essentially set up as a circle with the center of the city showcasing the Duomo and having every shopping brands you can imagine. In the north the areas of Brera and Isola are more artsy and modern areas having many rooftop lounges and great cafes. At night don’t forget to take a trip down to Navigli. This canal area is sprawling with bars and people at all hours of the day. If you are a foodie this also one of the best cities you can visit, having amazing pizza, pasta, and other Milanese food at almost every corner. Dinners also happen quite late in Milan, and all of Italy, with an apretivo as a bite before dinner. Apretivo is a 9 euro all you can eat buffet that is meant to be a snack but can be your dinner if you’re so inclined. Known for being a fashion capital, Milan is that and so much more. Check out what all you can do in this amazing city below!
