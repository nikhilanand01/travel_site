Budapest

At A Glance

Kiraly utca and Erzsébetváros are some of the liveliest areas in the Budapest, home to trendy bars, vibrant clubs, hip restaurants, and rustic street food. Being outside of the EU Budapest uses Hungarian Forints instead of euros. Being a very cheap city compared to the rest of Europe you are bound to get a good bang for your buck. For perspective 30000HUF (~$100USD) should be good for about 3 days of activities. Getting around Budapest with public transportation is quite easy, but if you want to use a ride hailing app you must download Bolt. Bolt is the Uber of Hungary with decent prices to get almost everywhere. Check out what all you can do in this amazing city below!
