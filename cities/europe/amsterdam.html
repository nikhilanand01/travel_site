Amsterdam

At A Glance

The city of Amsterdam is set up around its inner, middle, and outer canals. The city center is all inside the inner canal ring. As soon as you step outside the Central station you will be in the heart of the city with the Dam Square, Shopping, Coffeeshops, and the Red Light District all right there. Moving farther out the center, Leidseplein and Rembrandtplein are where you want to be if you’re looking for a fun night out. The south houses the city’s museum district, Museumplein, and the Albert Cuyp Market, where you can get some of the best Stroopwaffels and Poffertjes in the world. Biking is the most common transportation method, but with fantastic public transportation, it very easy to get anywhere in the city via tram.
