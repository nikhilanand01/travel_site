Prague

At A Glance

The Old Town of Prague is the main city center area and where most of the nightlife happens. Being outside of the EU, Prague uses Czech Crowns instead of euros. Being a very cheap city compared to the rest of Europe you are bound to get a good bang for your buck. For perspective, 2000CZK (~$85USD) should be good for about 3 days of activities. In the day Prague has many historical sites to see, and at night a lot of places to go and enjoy. Being a relatively small city walking can get you almost everywhere walking, but there is good public transport or cheap Ubers, UberPop, if you don’t feel like walking.
