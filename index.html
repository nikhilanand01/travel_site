• 5° Travel

The Name: Our site names comes from the concept of 6 degrees of separation. 6DOS is the idea that any one person can get in contact with anyone in a maximum of six connections, but it has now become 5 because of the social media. The idea of traveling can seem intimidating, but no matter where you go chances are you know someone who has been there or lives in that city. And if you don’t, it’s a great way to meet new people and widen your circles.
Also, 6 degrees was already taken, so thanks to Facebook for taking away 1 degree :)

The Concept: When going to a new city, there can be a lot to see and do, and it can be a lot of work to figure out what all that is. These guides are here to be your one-stop shop of what all the city has to offer. It's not meant to be an itinerary, but a guide to show what the best options are. For each city, we give you the markers and then it's your job to color-in as you see fit.
